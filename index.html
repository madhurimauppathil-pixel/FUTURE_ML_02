Analysis & Recommendations
observations from priority classification results
⚠
Challenge
Priority is contextual — the same ticket type (e.g. Billing) spans High/Medium/Low based on urgency cues that are hard to extract from short ticket text alone.
✦
Strength
Low priority achieves the best F1 at 52%, likely due to distinctive keywords like "request", "suggestion", "question" that consistently signal low urgency.
→
Next Steps
Consider semantic embeddings (BERT / SentenceTransformers) or ensemble stacking with predicted category as an additional feature for priority inference.
